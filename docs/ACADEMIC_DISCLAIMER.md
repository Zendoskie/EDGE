# Academic Use Disclaimer (Research Documentation)

This document provides formal disclaimer language for the EDGE research paper, thesis chapters, methodology sections, and system evaluation reports. The same text is implemented in the EDGE web application.

## System disclaimer (full text)

This system is designed solely to provide academic risk indicators and predictive insights based on available academic data. It does not diagnose, determine, or identify the specific causes of a student’s academic difficulties. The results generated by the system are intended only as decision-support information and should not replace the professional judgment, guidance, assessment, or intervention of teachers, advisers, counselors, or other academic personnel.

## Prediction results reminder (short form)

Predictions are intended for academic support purposes only.

---

## Suggested placement in the research paper

### Abstract or introduction (one sentence)

State that EDGE outputs academic risk *signals* for early support and explicitly disclaims diagnostic or causal attribution regarding student difficulties.

### Limitations of the study

Include the following points in formal academic prose:

1. **Scope of outputs.** The system produces risk classifications and recommendations derived from enrollment, attendance, assessment, and related academic records. These outputs are probabilistic indicators, not clinical, psychological, or causal diagnoses.

2. **Non-attribution of causes.** The system does not identify why a student is struggling (for example, personal, health, financial, or instructional factors). Any interpretation of underlying causes remains the responsibility of qualified academic personnel.

3. **Decision-support role.** Instructors, advisers, and counselors retain authority over interventions. The system must not be treated as a substitute for professional judgment, advising conferences, counseling referrals, or institutional policy.

4. **Data dependency.** Risk signals reflect the quality and completeness of data entered into the platform. Missing or delayed records may affect predictions.

### Ethical considerations

Note adherence to institutional policies on student data privacy, informed use of analytics in academic settings, and transparency with students regarding how academic performance data inform risk indicators.

### System interface

Reference that end users encounter the full disclaimer at login, on the dashboard, and on pages displaying predictions and performance insights, consistent with responsible deployment of educational technology.
